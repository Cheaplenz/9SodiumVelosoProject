InnovatePH: Sustainable Industry For All

Advocacy for SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

Introduction

Welcome to InnovatPH! This website is dedicated to advocating for SDG 9: Build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization and foster innovation.

Brief Background of the Problem

  • Globally, over 2.3 billion people lack access to basic sanitation and infrastructure services (UN, 2023).
  • In developing countries, industries are often not environmentally sustainable, leading to pollution and resource depletion.
  • According to the World Bank, 60% of global greenhouse gas emissions come from industrial activities.
  • In the Philippines, challenges include poor transport infrastructure, insufficient internet connectivity, and underdeveloped manufacturing sectors.
  • The Asian Development Bank (ADB, 2022) reported that the Philippines needs $127 billion worth of infrastructure investment by 2030 to keep up with economic growth.

What is being done about it in the Philippines?

  • The government is addressing these through programs like “Build, Build, Build”, which focused on roads, airports, and bridges.
  • The National Innovation Council (NIC) was created to foster innovation and research.
  • The Department of Science and Technology (DOST) supports small businesses with technology-based solutions.

Purpose of the Website

  • Educate people about SDG 9.
  • Highlight the importance of sustainable infrastructure and innovation.
  • Showcase the Philippines’ efforts.
  • Encourage youth involvement in building a better future.
